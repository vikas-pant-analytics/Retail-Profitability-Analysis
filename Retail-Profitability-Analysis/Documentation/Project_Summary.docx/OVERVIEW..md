\# Retail Profitability \& Discount Analysis Dashboard



Created By: Vikas Pant  

Role: Aspiring Data Analyst



\---



\## Project Objective

This dashboard analyzes retail sales, profitability, and discount trends to identify loss-making products, profitable categories, and regional performance patterns.



\---



\## Tools Used

\- Google Sheets

\- SQL

\- Power BI



\---



\## Key KPIs

\- Total Profit

\- Profit Margin

\- Average Discount

\- Loss Transactions



\---



\## Dashboard Insights

\- Higher discounts are associated with lower profitability.

\- Copiers and Phones are the most profitable sub-categories.

\- Tables and Bookcases generated major losses.

\- West region showed the highest overall profit.

\- Consumer segment contributed the highest profit share.



\---



\## Recommendations

\- Reduce excessive discounts on low-performing products.

\- Focus sales strategy on high-profit categories.

\- Improve performance in low-profit regions.

\- Use data-driven pricing strategies to increase profitability.
